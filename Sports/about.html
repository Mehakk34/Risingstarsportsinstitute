Our Institute Logo

Our School logo is made up of a pair of green and maroon slashes. The slashes pair is a universal symbol of flight and is used to embody the soaring
aspirations of our student-athletes. The waves of the slashes denotes the striking of a balance between academic finesse and sporting excellence
of our sportsman.

The colour Green signifies the passion and pride of our student-athletes and staff, while Maroon represents their youthfulness and vigour.

Outlined amidst the pair of wings is a trophy. This symbolises the School's commitment in providing our student-athletes the environment and ample
opportunities to help them fulfil their sporting potential and let their dreams take flight.The Stars at the top signifies our young achievers who raises
our standards high.

The school logo is a registered trade mark of Rising Stars Sports Institute.
