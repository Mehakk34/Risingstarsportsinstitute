Careers


If you are a highly-motivated and engaging individual who wants to contribute to the holistic development of high-performing student-athletes,
we invite you to join us.

TEACHER, ENGLISH LANGUAGE AND LITERATURE


You will be responsible for the teaching, learning and mentoring of our student-athletes. You may also be involved in curriculum design,
development of pedagogical strategies and assessment plans. Join us if you want to support the academic pursuit and holistic development of
our aspiring national athletes!


Requirements

  • Professional teaching qualification, such as the Postgraduate Diploma in Education from the National Institute of Education, or other similar qualifications
  • At least 3 years of teaching experience in secondary school or pre-university institution
  • Experience in preparing students for the GCE O-level, A-level or the International Baccalaureate Diploma is preferred
  • A good undergraduate degree in a relevant discipline is preferred
  • Interest in sports preferred

COACH, TRACK & FIELD ACADEMY


You will be responsible for planning and implementing the training and competition plans for a group of student-athletes towards high performance in sports, based on long-term athlete development principles, under the guidance of the Track & Field Academy Head Coach. You will primarily be coaching sprints, hurdles and relays, but you should have a good knowledge, understanding and experience in fundamental movement skills and multi-event coaching in all events groups for U16 student-athletes. You will keep track of the progress of the student-athletes and provide regular updates to the Track & Field Academy, the student-athletes and their parents. You will work with the Head Coach, General Manager, fellow coaches, school staff and external stakeholders to achieve the sporting goals and holistic development of the student-athletes under your care.


If you are a highly motivated, engaging and committed team player with a good understanding of the needs of elite student-athletes, and possess the competencies and passion to develop student-athletes to be competitive athletes, we invite you to join us.


Requirements

  • At least 5 years of competitive coaching experience with elite and developmental youth athletes, especially in sprints, hurdles and relays and with knowledge, understanding and experience in fundamental movement skills and multi-event coaching in all events groups
  • Possesses an international coaching qualification in Track and Field Athletics recognised or accredited by World Athletics, of at least Level 1 Coaching, with Level 2 Coaching preferred
  • Possesses at least SG-Coach Level 1 certification or equivalent and is registered with the National Registry of Coaches (NROC). For foreign coach selected for the position, Singapore Sports School will facilitate the registration with the NROC
  • Displays good understanding of sports science as applied to elite youth sports based on long term athlete development principles
  • Good planning, administrative and organisational skills

SENIOR MANAGER/ MANAGER, BOARDING OPERATIONS


You will provide strong leadership and support to the Boarding Operations. Your job responsibilities include providing direction and guidance to the Boarding Team in ensuring smooth boarding operations, and that the student-athletes’ overall safety, health and well-being are well looked after. You will coach and develop the Team to be effective role models and team players and to exercise their duty of care diligently. You will also ensure operational support to the meal and, laundry services, and review and improve all Boarding Standard Operating Procedures (SOP). You will lead in conceptualising, planning, implementing and evaluating Boarding programmes/ projects such as student-leadership, sustainable/ recycling projects at Boarding, and be involved in ad-hoc school-wide projects. You will lead and guide the Team in managing all incidents/ emergencies and provide timely updates to School Management. You are required to stay in Boarding during weekdays and be flexible in your working hours, including being called back for duties during emergencies.


Requirements

  • Have excellent leadership and management skills, particularly problem-solving, planning, organising, communication, and interpersonal skills
  • Able to lead teams effectively and forge strong partnerships with stakeholders to meet desired outcomes at department/school levels
  • Have a positive work attitude and drive to work independently and effectively as part of a team. • Have at least 5 years of relevant experience
  • Candidates with background in operations management, education, or counselling will be an advantage
  • Experience working in a school setting and/or a boarding or residential environment for young people will be advantageous
  • Successful candidate must reside in the Boarding School. Accommodation and meals will be provided

SENIOR EXECUTIVE/ EXECUTIVE, HUMAN RESOURCE


You will be involved in the Human Resource functions pertaining to recruitment, performance management, training and benefits administration. You will assist to review human resource policies and practices and implement new initiatives that contribute to organisational success. You will compile and analyse HR data and support in all other HR areas as assigned.


Requirements

  • At least 3 years of broad-based Human Resource experience
  • Good interpersonal, oral and written communication skills
  • A good team player, highly motivated and performance-driven
  • Good understanding of Singapore employment legislations
  • A degree in any discipline, preferably with post-graduate qualification in Human Resource

HOW TO APPLY


You should forward a detailed CV with a recent photograph, stating your academic/professional qualifications, working experience and current and expected salaries to risingstar@sportsinstitute.edu.sg. Please indicate the job title on the subject heading of your email. We regret that only shortlisted candidates will be notified.


Career opportunities for non-teaching positions can also be found on Jobstreet.com and MyCareersFuture.
